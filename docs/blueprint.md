# **App Name**: Fretboard Femmes

## Core Features:

- Artist Profiles: Browse artist profiles featuring biographies, images, and links to music and social media.
- Artist Search: Search for female guitarists by name, genre, or instrument.
- Discovery Feed: Curated discovery feed with a focus on introducing under-represented and new female guitarists, including a tool to make the descriptions more personable
- Favorites List: Save favorite guitarists to a personal collection for easy access.
- Listen Now: Link to stream and buy tracks or albums of discovered guitarists.
- View tour dates: View upcoming tour dates

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF), a vibrant hue suggestive of stage lights.
- Background color: Deep purple (#281E36), desaturated but noticeably related to the electric blue, that creates a rich, modern feel.
- Accent color: Metallic silver (#B4A6AB), provides visual interest, modernity and evokes the image of a guitar fret.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif with a precise, contemporary feel.
- Use modern, minimalist icons. Consider incorporating guitar-themed shapes (picks, soundwaves).
- Emphasize imagery. Ensure the profile images are in focus.
- Use subtle transitions and animations. On loading artist images, use fade-in animation.
