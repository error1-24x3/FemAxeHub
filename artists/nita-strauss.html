Biography
Nita Strauss is an American rock musician. She is the current touring guitarist for Alice Cooper and has a successful solo career, having been ranked No. 1 on Guitar World's list of "10 Female Guitar Players You Should Know". Her energetic and technical playing has made her a prominent figure in modern rock.
